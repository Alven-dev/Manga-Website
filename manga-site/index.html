What is manga?

Manga (漫画) is a style of Japanese comic books or graphic novels. It is a major part of Japanese pop culture and covers a wide range of genres, including action, adventure, romance, fantasy, horror, and more.

Key Features of Manga :

  • Origin : Japan
  • Reading Direction : Usually read from right to left
  • Art Style : Black-and-white drawings with expressive characters and dynamic panel layouts
  • Genres : Shōnen (for boys), Shōjo (for girls), Seinen (for adult men), Josei (for adult women), and many others
  • Publication : Released in magazines (e.g., Weekly Shōnen Jump) before being collected into volumes (tankōbon)
